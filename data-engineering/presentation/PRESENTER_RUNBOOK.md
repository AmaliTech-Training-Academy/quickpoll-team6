# QuickPoll Team 6 Presenter Runbook (15 Minutes)

## Timing Plan
- Target talk time: **13:30 to 14:00**
- Buffer for transition/slight delays: **1:00 to 1:30**
- Q&A: **5:00**

## Slide-by-Slide Script (Suggested)
- **Slide 1 (0:45)** - Open with business value and team composition.
- **Slide 2 (1:10)** - Explain the real collaboration pain in distributed teams.
- **Slide 3 (1:00)** - Walk through the decision loop and user journey.
- **Slide 4 (1:00)** - Prove MVP scope coverage at a glance.
- **Slide 5 (0:45)** - Set demo expectations and role handoffs.
- **Slide 6 (1:20)** - Architecture at business level: reliability, security, scalability.
- **Slide 7 (1:20)** - Data engineering impact and analytics readiness.
- **Slide 8 (1:10)** - QA + DevOps quality controls.
- **Slide 9 (1:00)** - Team cohesiveness and sprint execution model.
- **Slide 10 (1:00)** - Explicit evaluator scorecard mapping.
- **Slide 11 (1:20)** - Retrospective: wins, challenges, improvements.
- **Slide 12 (0:30)** - Close and transition to Q&A.

## Suggested Speaker Ownership
- **Frontend + Backend:** Slides 1-6 (product narrative + live flow)
- **Data Engineering:** Slide 7 (analytics pipeline + metrics value)
- **QA + DevOps:** Slide 8 (quality + deployment confidence)
- **Scrum/team representative:** Slides 9-11 (cohesion + retrospective)
- **Any presenter:** Slide 12 and Q&A moderation

## Live Demo Micro-Script (Slide 5)
1. Login with prepared demo account.
2. Open/create a poll with realistic options.
3. Cast vote (use second user if possible).
4. Show updated result context and engagement indicators.
5. Tie back to business impact: faster, auditable decisions.

## Q&A Cheat Sheet (Dimension-Aligned)
- **Sprint Goal Achievement:** "We delivered an integrated stack with working poll flow and analytics outputs."
- **User Experience:** "The demo follows a simple path from login to decision outcome with minimal friction."
- **Team Cohesiveness:** "Clear role ownership plus daily integration checkpoints reduced blockers."
- **Presentation Delivery:** "Data is preloaded and sequence rehearsed to stay inside 15 minutes."
- **Clarity & Structure:** "Narrative intentionally follows problem -> solution -> proof -> retrospective."

## Fast Fallbacks (If Demo Risk Appears)
- If environment lag happens: switch immediately to architecture + evidence slides and explain expected behavior.
- If one feature is unstable: show implemented flow steps and continue with analytics/quality proof.
- Keep one person dedicated to navigation while another speaks.
