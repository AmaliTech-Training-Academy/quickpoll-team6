# QuickPoll Team 6 - Demo Day Run-of-Show Checklist

## 24 Hours Before
- [ ] Rehearse full flow twice with timers.
- [ ] Confirm all presenters know their slide ownership.
- [ ] Verify demo credentials and seeded data are available.
- [ ] Open the final deck and check layout/overflow once.

## 60 Minutes Before
- [ ] Start backend, frontend, and required services.
- [ ] Verify login works for demo accounts.
- [ ] Pre-create one backup poll in case live create step fails.
- [ ] Keep `QuickPoll_Team6_Presentation.pptx` open locally.

## 15 Minutes Before
- [ ] Join meeting early.
- [ ] Close unrelated apps/tabs to avoid distractions.
- [ ] Confirm screen sharing and audio quality.
- [ ] Assign one backup navigator.

## During Presentation
- [ ] Keep to timing targets per slide.
- [ ] Verbally map evidence to evaluator dimensions.
- [ ] Maintain clear handoffs between speakers.
- [ ] If demo issue occurs, move to fallback slides immediately.

## During Q&A
- [ ] Lead with concise answer, then provide evidence.
- [ ] Tie answers to business value first, technical details second.
- [ ] Highlight next-step improvements confidently.

## After Session
- [ ] Capture evaluator feedback verbatim.
- [ ] Log actionable improvements for post-sprint follow-up.
